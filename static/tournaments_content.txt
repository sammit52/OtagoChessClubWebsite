<h3 class="header"><b>Otago Under 13 Chess Championship 2023</b></h3>
<p>
<u><b>Date:</u></b> Saturday 5 August 2023
<br><br>
<u><b>Venue:</u></b> Otago Chess Club, 7 Maitland Street, Dunedin (by ‘The Oval’ playing fields)
<br><br>
<u><b>Director:</u></b> The Tournament Director will be Quentin Johnson (Otago Chess Club Junior Development Officer)
<br><br>
<u><b>Eligibility:</u></b> This event is open to all primary school players (Years 1– 8 inclusive) or players under 13 at 1 January 2023. Players may enter as individuals or as a team of three players from the same school. Parents of children under 8 years old must contact the DOP before they enter their child in the event.
<br><br>
<u><b>Format:</u></b> Six games in total, limit of 20 minutes thinking time per player in each game.
<br><br>
<u><b>Schedule:</u></b> Players’ meeting at 1.00pm. NB Late entries (after 1.00pm) may not be accepted. Prize-giving shortly after 5.00pm. Parents/caregivers are requested to be at the venue by 5.15pm
<br><br>
<u><b>Prize Fund:</u></b> There will also be prizes for leading place holders as well as special prizes including the best girl and best school team.
<br><br>
<u><b>Entry Fees:</u></b> Individual entry fee: $15.00
<br><br>
<u><b>Team entry fee (3 players):</u></b> $30.00
<br><br>

<p>
<u><b>Date:</u></b> Saturday 19 August 2023
<br><br>
<u><b>Venue:</u></b> Otago Chess Club, 7 Maitland Street, Dunedin (by ‘The Oval’ playing fields)
<br><br>
<u><b>Format:</u></b> 6 Round Swiss, rapid time limit of 25 minutes for the game.
<br><br>
<u><b>Schedule:</u></b> Players’ meeting at 12.00pm. Late entries (after 12.00pm) may not be accepted. Play will commence 12.30pm. Prize-giving shortly after 5.50pm. All parents/caregivers are asked to be at the venue by 6.00pm.

<br><br>
<u><b>Eligibility:</u></b> This event is open to all juniors (under 20 on 1 January 2023) residing in the Otago/Southland region. Players may enter as individuals or as a team of three players from the same school. Parents of children under 10 must contact the DOP before they enter their child in the event.
<br><br>
<u><b>Prize Fund:</u></b> The prize fund will be contingent on entries. Cash prizes to Otago/Southland title-holders (Open, Under 16, Under 13 champions). There will also be special prizes which will include the best girl and best school team.
